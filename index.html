About Us

We are a team of College Students looking to provide educational opportunities to the K-12 community

Our tutoring services were spawned out of a need to give back to our community during the crisis we are facing today. Following the COVID-19 fallout and related school closures, it became quite apparent that our school-age children weren't getting the education that they otherwise should.

In an effort to ensure school-age members of the community were still able to get learning opportunities, we started Future Minds. Offering tracks in math, english, and computer science, we hope to give our children access to supplemental educational resources.

Our tutoring philosophy combines group learning with one-on-one instruction to ensure that each student receives individual attention as well as an opportunity to interact with peers. Given COVID-19, our classes will be 100% online.

Our Courses

All courses run from the week of June 8th to the week of July 27th
Each class meets twice a week: once as a group (60 min), and once individually (25 min)

Mathematics

We offer two tracks in math. Our K-5 track covers the basic number systems, number formats, and works up to introductory algebra. Our 6-8 track covers pre-algebra up to pre-calculus

English

Our english track focuses on developing creative writing skills, with additional instruction in grammar, essay-style writing, and test-prep

Computer Science

Our Computer Science track is an introductory level course for all ages on the basics of C++. The content is foundational with a mix of lecture content and hands-on projects.
